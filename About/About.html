About Salon Lumii

At Lumii Salon, we are passionate about enhancing natural beauty and providing an exceptional beauty experience. With a focus on high-quality hair, skin, and nail services, our expert team is committed to delivering personalized treatments that leave you feeling rejuvenated and confident. Whether it's a refreshing facial, a stylish haircut, or creative nail art, Lumii Salon is dedicated to transforming your beauty journey with innovation and care.

Our Journey

Salon Lumii was established on May 10th, 2015, by Alex Carter, a seasoned Skin Care Specialist, with a commitment to providing top-tier beauty services. Our journey began with a passion for personalized skincare treatments, quickly earning us a reputation for excellence in the beauty industry. In 2018, we relocated to a more spacious and modern location at No. 546/B, Jackson Road, Moratuwa, Western Province, to better serve our growing clientele and offer an enhanced, luxurious experience.

Today, Salon Lumii continues to evolve, offering a wide range of beauty treatments with a focus on skincare and transformative beauty experiences. Under the leadership of Salon Manager Alex Carter, our dedicated team ensures that every client receives the best care, leaving them feeling rejuvenated and confident.
